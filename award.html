Awards & Achievements of
Loyola International Public School

Celebrating Excellence

Loyola International Public School takes pride in its consistent achievements in academics, sports, cultural activities, and co-curricular competitions. Our students and faculty continue to earn recognition at district, state, and national levels through dedication, discipline, and excellence. Every achievement reflects our commitment to nurturing confident, responsible, and successful future leaders.

  • Academic Achievements

    Students consistently secure outstanding results in CBSE examinations with excellent academic performance and subject distinctions.

  • Sports Excellence

    Our students have won medals and championships in various district, state, and inter-school sports competitions.

  • Cultural & Co-Curricular Success

    Outstanding performances in debates, arts, music, dance, science exhibitions, and talent competitions.

  • Recognition & Awards

    Recognized for excellence in education, innovation, leadership, and holistic student development through prestigious awards and accolades.

About Us

Loyola International Public School provides quality education in a safe, modern and value-based learning environment. We nurture students with academic excellence, discipline, leadership and moral values to prepare them for a successful future.
