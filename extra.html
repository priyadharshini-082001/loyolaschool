BEYOND ACADEMICS

Activities That Inspire
Learning Beyond the Classroom

At Loyola International Public School, students are encouraged to explore their talents beyond academics through sports, arts, cultural activities, leadership programmes, and skill development initiatives that nurture confidence, creativity, discipline, and holistic growth.

Carrom

Develop concentration, patience and strategic thinking through exciting indoor carrom games and competitions.

Chess

Improve analytical thinking, problem-solving, decision-making and strategic planning through chess training.

Dance

Encourage creativity, confidence and cultural appreciation through classical and contemporary dance programmes.

Music & Drums

Discover rhythm, musical expression and performance skills through instrumental and percussion training.

Karate

Build self-confidence, physical fitness, discipline and self-defense skills under expert guidance.

No Plastic Campaign

Inspire students to become environmentally responsible by promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices.

Skating

Enhance balance, agility, coordination and endurance while enjoying professional skating sessions.

Silambam

Preserve India's traditional martial art while improving strength, discipline, focus and confidence.

Sports & Games

Foster teamwork, leadership, fitness and sportsmanship through indoor and outdoor sporting activities.

Yoga

Promote physical fitness, mental well-being, flexibility, and concentration through regular yoga practice and mindfulness activities.

About Us

Loyola International Public School provides quality education in a safe, modern and value-based learning environment. We nurture students with academic excellence, discipline, leadership and moral values to prepare them for a successful future.
