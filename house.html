House System

Home Club Activity

Red House

The Red House symbolizes courage, determination, and confidence. Students are encouraged to develop leadership qualities, teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship through active participation in various inter-house competitions and school events.

  • Leadership Development
  • Sports Competitions
  • Cultural Events
  • Academic Activities
  • Community Service
  • Team Building Programmes

Blue House

The Blue House represents wisdom, discipline, and responsibility. Students work together to achieve excellence in academics, sports, literary competitions, and various co-curricular activities.

  • Quiz Competitions
  • Science Activities
  • Sports Events
  • Debates & Elocution
  • Leadership Programmes
  • House Meetings

Green House

The Green House encourages environmental awareness, teamwork, and creativity. Students actively participate in eco-friendly initiatives while developing responsibility towards nature and society.

  • Tree Plantation
  • Campus Cleanliness
  • Gardening Activities
  • Environmental Awareness
  • Recycling Projects
  • Nature Conservation

Yellow House

The Yellow House promotes enthusiasm, creativity, optimism, and innovation. Students are encouraged to participate in literary, cultural, artistic, and leadership activities throughout the academic year.

  • Essay Writing
  • Music & Dance
  • Drama & Theatre
  • Creative Arts
  • Public Speaking
  • Talent Competitions
  • House Celebrations
  • Leadership Activities

About Us

Loyola International Public School provides quality education in a safe, modern and value-based learning environment. We nurture students with academic excellence, discipline, leadership and moral values to prepare them for a successful future.
