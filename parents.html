Rules & Policies

Loyola International Public School believes that discipline, responsibility, respect, and integrity are the foundation of quality education. Our rules and policies are designed to provide a safe, secure, and student-friendly environment where every learner can grow academically, socially, and morally. By following these guidelines, students develop self-discipline, leadership qualities, and a strong sense of responsibility while contributing to a positive and harmonious school community.

Loyola International Public School follows a well-planned daily schedule to ensure effective learning and balanced student development. Students are expected to arrive on time and actively participate in all academic and co-curricular activities throughout the school day.

School Timings

School Hours:
Monday – Friday : 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM

The prescribed school uniform reflects discipline, equality, and pride in the institution. Students are expected to wear a clean and neat uniform every day along with their ID card, black shoes, and white socks.

School Uniform

Dress Code:
Neat Uniform • ID Card • Black Shoes • White Socks

Attendance

Attendance Rules

Regular attendance is essential for academic success and overall student development. Students are expected to attend school regularly and punctually throughout the academic year.

  • Students should attend school regularly and punctually.
  • Parents should inform the school in case of absence.
  • Medical leave should include a valid medical certificate.
  • Continuous absenteeism affects academic performance.
  • Students must complete missed classwork.
  • Attendance is considered during overall evaluation.
Leave

Leave Rules

Students requiring leave should obtain prior approval from the school whenever possible. Parents should submit leave applications with valid reasons.

  • Leave should be requested in advance.
  • Medical leave requires supporting documents.
  • Extended leave requires Principal approval.
  • Complete missed assignments after returning.
  • Avoid leave during examinations.
  • Unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action.
Examination

Examination Rules

The school follows a transparent examination system based on CBSE guidelines to ensure fairness and academic excellence.

  • Report to the examination hall on time.
  • Bring all required writing materials.
  • Maintain discipline during examinations.
  • Malpractice is strictly prohibited.
  • Absence must be supported by valid reasons.
  • Follow all examination instructions.
Discipline

School Discipline

Discipline is one of the core values of Loyola International Public School. Students are expected to demonstrate honesty, respect and responsibility.

  • Respect teachers, staff and fellow students.
  • Wear the prescribed school uniform.
  • Maintain punctuality and attendance.
  • Protect school property.
  • Follow classroom and campus rules.
  • Use polite and respectful language.
  • Display good conduct everywhere.
  • Participate positively in school activities.
Parents

Parents Guidelines

Parents play a vital role in every child's educational journey. Strong cooperation between parents and the school ensures holistic development.

  • Ensure regular attendance and punctuality.
  • Monitor your child's academic progress.
  • Attend Parent-Teacher Meetings regularly.
  • Encourage discipline and positive values.
  • Inform the school about health concerns.
  • Communicate with teachers when necessary.
  • Support school policies and programmes.
  • Work together for your child's success.

About Us

Loyola International Public School provides quality education in a safe, modern and value-based learning environment. We nurture students with academic excellence, discipline, leadership and moral values to prepare them for a successful future.
